About us

Who we are

Odushu Foundation is a registered charity dedicated to the advancement of education, health, and welfare of children with a particular focus on boys who are disadvantaged or at risk. We believe every boy deserves a fair chance at a fulfilling future.

Governed by a board of at least three elected trustees and driven by a passionate membership open to all, we manage our funds responsibly and deliver targeted programs for real change.

  • Integrity in all we do
  • Child-centered approach
  • Community partnership
  • Transparency & accountability
What we do

Our Core Programs

Every program is designed specifically for the needs of boys, recognizing the unique challenges they face and building on their innate strengths.

Education & Skills Training

Providing essential education, academic resources, and practical skills training to disadvantaged boys, equipping them with the knowledge and practical abilities needed to thrive in today's world.

  • School supply distribution
  • Vocational skills training
  • Digital literacy programs
  • Scholarship pathways

Mental Health Initiatives

Facilitating tailored psychological support, safe spaces, and awareness initiatives to help boys navigate emotional challenges, build resilience, and develop healthy coping mechanisms.

  • Counselling & therapy access
  • Stress & trauma workshops
  • Safe space peer support groups
  • Awareness campaigns against stigmas

Mentorship Programs

Connecting at-risk boys with positive male role models who provide guidance, inspiration, and dedicated time to help them discover their potential, set goals, and navigate life's challenges.

  • One-on-one mentorship matching
  • Leadership character development
  • Career guidance & networking
  • Alumni success networks
