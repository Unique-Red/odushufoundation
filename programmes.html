Our Programmes

Comprehensive initiatives designed to empower boys through education, mentorship, and holistic support

Our Core Programmes

Each program is carefully designed to address specific needs and create lasting positive change in the lives of boys

Education & Skills Training

Our education program provides comprehensive academic support and vocational training to ensure every boy has the opportunity to succeed academically and develop practical skills for future employment.

Academic Tutoring

Free after-school tutoring in core subjects including mathematics, science, language arts, and more. Small group sessions ensure personalized attention.

Vocational Skills Training

Hands-on training in carpentry, electrical work, computer skills, and other trades. programmes include certification opportunities and job placement support.

Scholarship programmes

Financial assistance for school fees, uniforms, books, and supplies. Merit-based and need-based scholarships available for deserving students.

STEM Programmes

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics enrichment programmes including robotics clubs, coding workshops, and science fairs.

Mental Health Support

We recognize that mental and emotional well-being are crucial for overall development. Our mental health programmes provide comprehensive support through counseling, therapy, and wellness activities.

Individual Counselling

One-on-one sessions with licensed counselors who specialize in child and adolescent psychology. Confidential and safe space for boys to express themselves.

Group Therapy Sessions

Peer support groups where boys can share experiences and learn from each other. Topics include anxiety management, anger control, and building resilience.

Wellness Workshops

Regular workshops on stress management, emotional intelligence, mindfulness, and healthy coping strategies. Includes art therapy and music therapy sessions.

Crisis Intervention

24/7 crisis hotline and emergency response services for boys facing acute mental health challenges or traumatic situations.

Mentorship Programmes

Our mentorship initiative connects boys with positive role models who provide guidance, support, and inspiration. These relationships help boys develop confidence, set goals, and navigate life's challenges.

One-on-One Mentoring

Carefully matched pairs of mentors and mentees who meet regularly for activities, conversations, and guidance. Long-term relationships that foster trust and growth.

Career Mentorship

Industry professionals mentor boys interested in specific career paths, providing insight, networking opportunities, and real-world experience.

Leadership Development

Programmes that build leadership skills, public speaking ability, and community engagement. Older mentees often become peer mentors themselves.

Group Activities

Regular group outings, sports activities, cultural events, and community service projects that strengthen bonds and build teamwork skills.
